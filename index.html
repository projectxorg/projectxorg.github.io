projectx Kodi addon

projectx addon is an addon for Kodi, that manages your virtual library, syncs with your Trakt account . Author does not take any responsibility for how users use this software!

Addons plugin.video.projectx and script.projectx.burst are not supported!

  • Do not expect any support, help and other things like that.
  • Source is open, so you can fork everything.
